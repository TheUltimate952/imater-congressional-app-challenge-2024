Time: 50-60 minutes

City: St. Augustine

Ingredients
  • 1 pre-made pie crust (9-inch)
  • 1 cup sour orange juice (or a mix of orange and lime juice)
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Whipped cream (for topping, optional)
    •

Sour Orange Pie

Preparation
  1. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Prepare the filling: In a mixing bowl, whisk together the sour orange juice, granulated sugar, eggs, melted butter, flour, and vanilla extract until smooth and well combined.
  3. Fill the crust: Pour the filling into the pre-made pie crust, spreading it evenly.
  4. Bake the pie: Bake in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes, or until the filling is set and the top is lightly golden.
  5. Cool and serve: Allow the pie to cool to room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving. Top with whipped cream if desired.
