Time: 45 minutes

City: Orlando

Ingredients
  • 1 baguette (preferably Vietnamese or French)
  • 1/2 pound cooked pork (grilled pork, pork belly, or pâté)
  • 1/2 cup pickled daikon radish (and carrots, optional)
  • Fresh cucumber, sliced
  • Fresh cilantro sprigs
  • Jalapeño or Thai chili peppers, sliced (optional)
  • Mayonnaise
  • Soy sauce or Maggi seasoning (optional)
    •

Banh Mi Sandwich

Preparation
  1. Prepare the pickles: If not using store-bought, pickle daikon radish and carrots by soaking them in a mixture of vinegar, sugar, and salt for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Prepare the bread: Slice the baguette lengthwise, being careful not to cut all the way through. Lightly toast it if desired.
  3. Spread the sauce: Spread a generous layer of mayonnaise on both sides of the baguette. Drizzle with soy sauce or Maggi seasoning if using.
  4. Add the fillings: Layer the cooked pork (or your choice of protein) inside the baguette. Top with pickled daikon and carrots, sliced cucumber, fresh cilantro, and jalapeño or Thai chili peppers if desired.
  5. Close and serve: Close the sandwich, pressing it gently to combine the flavors. Slice in half and serve immediately.
