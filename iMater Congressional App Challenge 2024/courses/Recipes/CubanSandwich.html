Appetizers Entrees Desserts

Time: 16-20 minutes

City: Miami

Ingredients
  • 1 loaf Cuban bread (or soft French bread)
  • 1/2 pound roast pork, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 pound ham, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 pound Swiss cheese, sliced
  • 1/4 cup dill pickles, sliced
  • Yellow mustard
  • Butter (for grilling)
    •

Cuban Sandwich

Preparation
  1. Prepare the bread: Slice the Cuban bread lengthwise, leaving one side attached (like a hinge). If using French bread, slice it open.
  2. Assemble the sandwich: Spread yellow mustard on the inside of both sides of the bread. Layer the roast pork, ham, Swiss cheese, and sliced pickles inside the bread.
  3. Grill the sandwich: Preheat a skillet or panini press over medium heat. Spread butter on the outside of the bread. Place the sandwich in the skillet or press, and cook for about 3-5 minutes on each side, or until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  4. Serve: Remove the sandwich from the heat, slice in half diagonally, and serve immediately.
