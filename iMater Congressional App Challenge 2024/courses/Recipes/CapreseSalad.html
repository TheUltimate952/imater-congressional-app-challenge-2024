Appetizers Entrees Desserts

Time: 10 minutes

City: Ft. Lauderdale

Ingredients
  • 3-4 large ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Freshly cracked black pepper to taste
    •

Caprese Salad

Preparation
  1. Prepare the ingredients: Slice the tomatoes and fresh mozzarella cheese into even slices.
  2. Assemble the salad: On a serving platter, alternate layers of tomato slices and mozzarella slices. Tuck fresh basil leaves between the layers.
  3. Drizzle with dressing: Drizzle extra virgin olive oil and balsamic vinegar (if using) over the salad.
  4. Season: Sprinkle salt and freshly cracked black pepper to taste.
  5. Serve: Serve immediately as a fresh appetizer or side dish.
