Appetizers Entrees Desserts

Time: 35 minutes

City: Jacksonville

Ingredients
  • 2 large green tomatoes, sliced into 1/4-inch thick rounds
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tablespoon Cajun seasoning
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)
    •

Fried Green Tomatoes with Shrimp

Preparation
  1. Prepare the tomatoes: Dip the sliced green tomatoes in buttermilk, then dredge in a mixture of cornmeal, flour, salt, black pepper, and cayenne pepper (if using). Ensure they are well coated.
  2. Fry the tomatoes: Heat vegetable oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the coated green tomato slices in batches. Fry for about 3-4 minutes on each side, until golden brown. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  3. Cook the shrimp: In the same skillet, add a little more oil if needed. Season the shrimp with Cajun seasoning and sauté for about 2-3 minutes until they are pink and cooked through. Remove from heat.
  4. Assemble the dish: On a plate, layer the fried green tomatoes and top with sautéed shrimp.
  5. Prepare the remoulade sauce (optional): Serve with your favorite remoulade sauce drizzled over the top or on the side for dipping.
  6. Garnish and serve: Optionally garnish with fresh parsley and serve immediately.
