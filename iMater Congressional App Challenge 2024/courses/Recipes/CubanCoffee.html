Appetizers Entrees Desserts

Time: 5 minutes

City: Miami

Ingredients
  • 1 cup finely ground dark roast coffee (Cuban coffee or espresso)
  • 1 cup water
  • 1-2 tablespoons sugar (to taste)
  • 1-2 tablespoons evaporated milk (optional)
    •

Cuban Coffee

Preparation
  1. Brew the coffee: In a stovetop espresso maker or coffee pot, add the finely ground coffee and water. Brew according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  2. Prepare the sugar: While the coffee is brewing, place the sugar in a small cup. Once the coffee is brewed, pour a small amount (about 1-2 ounces) of the coffee over the sugar to create a paste. Whisk until it becomes a creamy consistency.
  3. Mix the coffee: Once the coffee is fully brewed, pour the remaining coffee into the cup with the sugar paste. Stir well to combine and dissolve the sugar completely.
  4. Serve: If desired, add evaporated milk for creaminess. Serve in small cups and enjoy!
