Appetizers Entrees Desserts

Time: 2-3 hours

City: Key West

Ingredients
  • 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs (about 10-12 full crackers)
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1 can (14 ounces) sweetened condensed milk
  • ½ cup key lime juice (fresh or bottled)
  • 1 tablespoon lime zest (optional)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2-3 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Key Lime Pie Bars

Preparation
  1. Prepare the crust: In a medium bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, granulated sugar, and melted butter. Mix well to coat the crumbs evenly.
  2. Press the crust: Firmly press the crumb mixture into the bottom of a parchment-lined 8x8-inch baking pan, ensuring an even layer. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 8-10 minutes until lightly golden. Set aside to cool.
  3. Make the filling: In a large bowl, whisk the egg yolks for about 2-3 minutes until thickened. Gradually add the sweetened condensed milk, whisking continuously. Stir in the key lime juice and lime zest (if using), mixing until smooth.
  4. Pour the filling: Once the crust is cooled, pour the lime filling over it and spread it evenly to cover the surface.
  5. Bake the bars: Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 15-18 minutes, until the filling is set but slightly jiggly in the center. Allow the bars to cool to room temperature, then refrigerate for 2-3 hours until firm.
  6. Make the whipped cream: In a large bowl, beat the heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until soft peaks form.
  7. Top and serve: Spread or pipe the whipped cream over the chilled bars. Garnish with lime zest if desired. Slice into squares and serve chilled.
