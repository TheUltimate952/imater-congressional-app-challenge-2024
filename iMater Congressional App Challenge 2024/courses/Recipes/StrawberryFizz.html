Appetizers Entrees Desserts

Time: 10 minutes

City: Plant City

Ingredients
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 cup sparkling water or club soda
  • Ice cubes
  • Fresh mint leaves (for garnish, optional)
    •

Strawberry Fizz

Preparation
  1. Prepare the strawberry mixture: In a bowl, combine the sliced strawberries, granulated sugar, and lemon juice. Muddle gently with a fork or muddler to release the juices from the strawberries. Let it sit for about 10 minutes to allow the sugar to dissolve and the flavors to meld.
  2. Assemble the drink: Fill a glass with ice cubes. Spoon the strawberry mixture over the ice, including some of the juices.
  3. Add the fizz: Pour the sparkling water or club soda over the strawberry mixture, stirring gently to combine.
  4. Garnish and serve: Garnish with fresh mint leaves if desired. Serve immediately and enjoy your refreshing Strawberry Fizz!
