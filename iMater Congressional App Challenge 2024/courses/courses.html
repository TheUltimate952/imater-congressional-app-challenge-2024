Appetizers

RECIPES

Caprese Salad

A fresh, light Italian salad made with ripe tomatoes, mozzarella, basil, and drizzled with olive oil and balsamic glaze.

Alligator Bites

Tender, bite-sized pieces of alligator meat, seasoned and fried to a golden crisp, often served with a dipping sauce.

Fried Green Tomatoes with Shrimp

Crispy fried green tomatoes topped with shrimp and a tangy, flavorful remoulade sauce for a Southern-inspired bite.

Crab Cakes

Golden, crispy patties filled with sweet crab meat, breadcrumbs, and seasonings, typically served with tartar or remoulade sauce.

Tzatziki

A creamy Greek dip made from yogurt, cucumber, garlic, and herbs, perfect for pairing with pita or fresh vegetables.

Strawberry Fizz

A refreshing drink with muddled strawberries, sugar, lemon juice, and sparkling water for a sweet and fizzy beverage.

Cuban Coffee

A strong, sweet espresso shot brewed with sugar, offering a rich and bold flavor, commonly enjoyed as a pick-me-up.

Entrees

RECIPES

Yellowtail Sandwich

A crispy, fried yellowtail fillet served on a soft bun with fresh toppings like lettuce, tomato, and tartar sauce.

Margherita Pizza

A classic Italian pizza topped with fresh mozzarella, tomatoes, basil, and olive oil on a crisp, thin crust.

Pho Noodle Soup

A traditional Vietnamese soup with rice noodles, beef or chicken, fresh herbs, and aromatic broth, garnished with lime and bean sprouts.

Banh Mi Sandwich

A Vietnamese sandwich with a crispy baguette filled with grilled meat, pickled vegetables, cilantro, and a tangy sauce.

Cuban Sandwich

A hearty sandwich made with Cuban bread, filled with roast pork, ham, Swiss cheese, pickles, and mustard, then pressed to perfection.

Cajun Blackened Redfish Recipe

A flavorful dish where redfish fillets are coated with Cajun spices and seared until blackened, served with rice or vegetables.

Fried Mahi Mahi

Lightly breaded and fried Mahi Mahi fillets, crispy on the outside and tender inside, often served with a tangy dipping sauce.

Greek Chicken Gyros

Warm pita bread filled with marinated grilled chicken, tzatziki sauce, fresh vegetables, and sometimes feta cheese.

Nicaraguan Carne Asada

Grilled, marinated beef steak seasoned with citrus and spices, served with tortillas and garnished with cilantro.

Gallo Pinto

A traditional Nicaraguan and Costa Rican dish of rice and black beans, sautéed with onions, peppers, and spices.

Desserts

RECIPES

Key Lime Pie Bars

Tangy and sweet key lime filling on a buttery graham cracker crust, cut into bars for a refreshing treat.

Tangerine Orange Cake Recipe

A light, moist cake infused with the sweet and citrusy flavor of tangerines and oranges, often topped with a citrus glaze.

Sour Orange Pie

A creamy and tart pie made with sour oranges, similar to key lime pie, on a graham cracker or pastry crust.

Florida Strawberry Shortcake

Layers of fresh, sweetened strawberries and whipped cream between fluffy shortcake biscuits for a summery dessert.
